Black Bear

A subspecies of Black Bears with white fur are known as Kermode.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus Americanus
  • Average Length: 4.5 ft
  • Average Lifespan: Item #3
  • Habitat: 10 to 30 years (conditions vary in number)

Black bears are quite the creature. They're able to climb very large trees and swim great distances. While they may be known as black bears, they come in different colors. Grey-blue, light-brown, and blond are some, just to name a few.
